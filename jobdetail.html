Psychologist

We are seeking a dedicated and compassionate Psychologist to join our college. As a Psychologist, you will play a pivotal role in providing mental health support and counseling services to individuals dealing with a variety of challenges.

Responsibility

Provide counseling services to college students dealing with academic, personal, or mental health issues. Conduct individual and group therapy sessions to address students' psychological concerns.

  • Conduct psychological assessments and provide therapy sessions
  • Develop personalized treatment plans for students.
  • Offer crisis intervention and support.
  • Collaborate with interdisciplinary teams for comprehensive care
  • Maintain accurate client records and Stay updated on psychology practices and research

Qualifications

A doctoral degree (Ph.D. or equivalent) in Psychology. Current licensure as a psychologist in the state of [State]. Experience providing psychotherapy and counseling services in a clinical setting. Strong assessment and diagnostic skills. Excellent communication and interpersonal skills. Ability to work collaboratively in a multidisciplinary team environment. Commitment to ethical principles and professional standards in psychology practice..

  • Doctoral degree in Psychology.
  • State licensure as a psychologist
  • Clinical experience in psychotherapy
  • Strong assessment and communication skills.
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a team and Commitment to ethical standards.
